Vision Statement

CATAPOL BIOSCIENCE Ltd. is a private limited company in Bangladesh which executes business in Animal Health Industry. We work here with passion, energy and urgency to enhance the health and productivity of food animals. Our purpose is to advance animal healthcare by bringing new medicines and services to Veterinarians, livestock procedures and aquaculture farmers in Bangladesh. Our work contributes to the food security value chain to create poverty free and hunger free Bangladesh.
